CALY-X | PORTFOLIO PROJECT #1

“Agriculture has, for many years, formed the backbone of Africa’s economy contributing 50%+ of the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and accounting for 80% of the employment, mainly in the rural areas. In addition, the sector contributes more than 75% of the total export earnings and about half of the tax revenue, while meeting the food requirements of the population. Africa’s agricultural sector directly influences overall economic performance and periods of high economic growth rates have been synonymous with increased agricultural growth. (Frontier Market Intelligence, tradeinvestafrica)”

The North Zambia Project

With the population of Sub-Saharan Africa expected to double by 2060, we are seeing a rapid, paramount growth of the food demand in the region. With food and agriculture commodity prices continuing to rise globally, the region is in danger of lapsing into a nutrition and economic crisis again, despite including some of the fastest-growing economies in the world, such as Kenya and Rwanda. That’s why there has never been a more important time to invest in the region.

We invite you to join our exclusive society, where, above everyone else in Calyx City, we will be able to invest in these honourable both profitable and worthwhile projects.

Acquisition of Land

We are proud to announce that our investment partners have already registered 99-year leases over some 25,000 hectares of well-watered land in Northern Zambia, with the possibility to double the hectarage.

The evolution and progression of humankind are at the core of Corporation X’s values. That’s why we want to invest in this profitable land, which, being one of the most stable in the region, sees unlimited growth potential, and by investment, support the local communities with agricultural infrastructure (such as associated power, storage, handling and processing), as well as medical and educational facilities, which altogether will create employment and an opportunity for sustainable development, as well as increase local wealth. Those advancements will not only provide resources to an under-resourced and underdeveloped region but will also do it in a fully sustainable and eco-friendly way.

Mungwi Farming Community

Our Northern Zambia Project will be based in the small town of Mungwi which neighbours Kasama, the capital of the Northern Province of Zambia. The location is particularly important for us because it is situated close to the shores of Lake Tanganyika which is a historically important region that was once a critical region in trade with the neighbours Democratic Republic of Congo, Tanzania and Malawi. Nowadays, with 30 years of democracy and stability in Zambia, as well as its good soil and reliable rainfall, the neighbouring countries are in need of additional food sources, the implications of which means rapid growth in demand for export and food production. That provides an unprecedented opportunity for investment.

Corporation X is proud to introduce its most elite members to Portfolio Project #1 and to participate in the renaissance of the Northern Zambia region, an important step in the quick evolution and technological advancement of humankind.

The Bemba Partnership

We are pleased to confirm that our partners are working closely with CRM Global, a company of specialists in global trade, consultancy and investment across the food, resource and agricultural sectors. CRM Global has established an official partnership with the Bemba Royal Establishment. and Mungwi lies within the Bemba curtilage. The project is set out working closely with His Royal Highness Chief Chitimukulu, the Paramount Chief and King of the Bemba. Through His Royal Highness, the nation of Bemba already holds an important shareholding and be actively involved in decisions making within the project.
