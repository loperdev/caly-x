THE CITY

Calyx City, a technologic utopia built on investment, power, invention and evolution. Amidst the escalating unrest between the oppressed lower districts and the technologic powerhouses sitting at the top, a brewing conflict starts to emerge over contrasting ideologies and clashing convictions.

CYPHER CREW

Architected on the sole purpose to take down Corporation - X. Founded in the lower districts of Calyx City, the group formed after the inhabitants of these districts were snatched for the DNA mutation trials. They pride themselves on building anything out of nothing and hacking into any mainframe in the city for information. A dangerous enemy to big technology corporations.

THE VARIANTS

The result of the DNA mutation trials carried on by the Corporation - X. There were 1000 subjects in total, but only 300 survived. The tests mutated an individual's appearance and physical traits. Each mutation was different depending on the person. The subjects that survived were labelled the perfect soldier by the Corporation - X, deemed to be the next step in human evolution. The ones that escaped back to the lower districts, went by the name The Variants.
