CRM Global Partners with Caly-X to Invest in Emerging Markets and Generate High-Yield Passive Income for Investors

BEMBA ROYAL ESTABLISHMENT

CALY-X has partnered with the Bemba Royal Establishment and Chitimukulu; the King (Paramount Chief) of the Bemba, the largest ethnic group in Zambia, with the objective of enabling farming to deliver maximum “farm gate” value in one of the most stable countries in Sub Saharan Africa and in a period of unquestionable food demand growth. CALY-X has already invested in fully registered 99 year leases over some 25,000 hectares of well watered land in Northern Zambia.There will be phased development of the land and critical services to enable each stage to support irrigated agriculture with associated power, storage, handling and processing. The project will be carbon neutral and ethical for locals.
