How does CALY-X CAPITAL™ work ?

Each CALY-X NFT of the 2023 mint shall equate to 2 shares in North Zambia Holdings Limited, the company behind Portfolio Project #1 | The North Zambia Project

However, CALY-X NFT holders can choose to go two directions with their NFT. As your investment, it is your choice. You can either hold your NFT, attaining the NFT as an asset in itself and also being applicable for revenue entitlement of Portfolio Project #1 | The North Zambia Project. The other option is to burn your NFT and execute on your options. This will exchange your NFT revenue entitlement (% of the net profit generated by the underlying physical business profit) for 2 shares (per NFT) in the North Zambia Holdings Limited. However, sacrificing your revenue entitlement will make you a shareholder in the company, so a part owner. But for this an NFT must be burned.

1 NFT Held = % of the net profit generated by the underlying physical business profit.

Your CALY-X share options for | Portfolio Project #1 | The North Zambia Project ( Link to Investments page) will expire on the 20th of March 2026. From that date your project will be fixed to receive revenue entitlement still, but your option to burn the NFT into 2 shares will now ‘not’ be applicable.

1 NFT Burned = 2 shares in North Zambia Holdings Limited.
